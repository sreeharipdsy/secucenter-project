What is SOC as a Staffing?

SOC-as-a-staffing is our solution designed for MSPs, MSSPs & IT departments looking to strengthen security with the assistance of dedicated, full-time SOC analyst. Our team of trained and certified SOC experts will work under your brand as an extension of your in-house team and follow your terms of work to provide quality security support coverage.

Our Approach

Secucenter aspires to give you the best and qualified talents as per your preference to suit your SOC requirements round the clock. Let us walk you through our approaches.

We provide you with the right talents to match your requirements after a proper screening process. We expedite a distinct training process, assess their performance until the end of the task and share a detailed report to keep you in loop.

SOC as a Staffing in Action

Quality Assurance

Completion and response time, first-time fix rate, client service level compliance, etc. are meticulously checked and conveyed to MSPs through a detailed QA report.

1 Manage

The incident watcher will monitor incidents at various touchpoints, analyze alerts, and categorize them to identify real and false alerts effectively.

2 Detect

If suspicious alerts are identified, tickets are raised to investigate the incident to understand the severity and accordingly inform the MSPs.

3 Respond

Mitigation suggestions will be communicated to the MSPs through detailed reports and the ticket will be closed upon receiving approval from the MSPs.

We adhere to the 3 main stages of SOC - manage, detect and respond - through well-defined processes which will be managed by staff from various tiers depending on the intensity of the function. The entire process then undergoes quality assurance to ensure everything is communicated to the MSPs at the right time in the best way.
